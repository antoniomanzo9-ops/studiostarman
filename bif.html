The Sentinel

Four layers between you and danger.

BIF analyzes every email through a weighted multi-layer scoring system. Typosquatting detection. Language manipulation analysis. Structural forensics. Behavioral context. The Sentinel never sleeps.

Layer 01
Domain

Levenshtein distance for typosquatting (p0ste.it vs poste.it). Leet-speak normalization. Fresh domain detection. TLD analysis against known Italian banks, couriers, and institutions.

Layer 02
Language

Urgency patterns. Fear triggers. Authority impersonation. Isolation tactics. 20+ heuristic signals calibrated for Italian phishing — because scams speak your language.

Layer 03
Structure

Masked links. Suspicious attachments. Sender/content mismatch. Header forgery. The anatomy of deception, laid bare.

Layer 04
Context

Time-of-day patterns. Frequency anomalies. Behavioral fingerprint. BIF knows what's normal for you — so it knows when something isn't.

The best security doesn't shout. It breathes. When you open BIF and see calm cyan, that calm is earned. Every second of peace is a second BIF spent watching for you.
