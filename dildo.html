Collection 01

Tools with a pulse, not templates.

This is the designer-facing room: small experimental engines, each built around one constraint. No polite preset packs. No Canva mood. Each system starts from an image, a rule, and a mistake worth keeping.

01. Mouth Grid2026

Set an image inside a moving grid and let the cells decide where the poster breathes.

02. Bleed2026

Push red, green and blue out of registration until the face turns into signal.

03. Echo2026

Cut movement into horizontal bands and keep the second before the body arrives.

04. Pool Mask2026

Generate wet masks from circles, cuts and impossible nightlife geometry.

05. Static Type2026

Turn a word into texture, then let the texture attack the photograph back.

06. Moire Room2026

Lay interference over a room until the image starts sounding like a speaker.
