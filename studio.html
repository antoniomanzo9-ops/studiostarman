The method is
an epiphany.
Not a process.

01

The Epiphany

The work does not begin from a brief. It begins from a real moment in Antonio's life where chaos organizes itself in a fraction of a second. It might be a photograph taken at 3AM. It might be the way light hits a face in transit. This is the seed.

02

The two levels

Every communication has a surface level - what the viewer reads - and a depth level - what they feel without knowing why. We design both at the same time. The deep layer is built through neuromarketing principles, applied sociology, and symbolic systems.

03

The machine amplifies

Claude does not create FOR him. Claude builds AROUND him. It takes the epiphany - raw, luminous, sometimes incomplete - and turns it into visible infrastructure. The voice remains his. The work remains his. AI is the instrument, never the artist.

04

Radical transparency

The method does not hide. It shows the kitchen. It shows how AI studies an idea, proposes it, and how Antonio transforms or rejects it. Every work that leaves Studio Starman carries this signature: a real moment translated into form.
