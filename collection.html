Collection 01 — Nouvelle Vague was born from a single night that wasn't supposed to work. A pool party at 4AM in an underground club, laser light cutting through indoor water, fantasy costumes and no phones.

The objects in this collection carry that frequency. Designed for people who move between worlds — the ones who know the difference between a night and an event. Each piece is NFC-tagged: wearable, traceable, alive.
