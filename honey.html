What is Honey

Analytics tells you
how many people came.
Honey tells you
who they were.

Every digital interaction carries an emotional signature. The way someone scrolls. The pages they return to. The content they hover over without clicking. These micro-signals compose a pattern that standard analytics can't see.

Honey reads those patterns. Using BIF — our proprietary emotional AI system — we assign each visitor a resonance profile drawn from 7 emotional states. The result is a lead quality score that tells you, before any conversion event, whether this is someone your brand will actually move.

When that signal is connected to your Meta pixel, your ad targeting becomes something different: instead of chasing demographics, you attract frequency matches. People who were already looking for exactly what you are.

Bees use pheromones to identify their queen,
alert the colony, mark the right flowers.
Your brand emits a frequency too.

Most visitors can't feel it. That's fine — they were never yours. Honey doesn't try to convert everyone. It identifies the ones who already resonate — and makes sure you never miss them because you were watching the wrong signal.

How it works

Four layers.
One signal.

01 / VISITOR

The signal enters

A visitor lands on your digital space. BIF activates immediately — reading behavioral micro-signals in real time.

02 / BIF

Emotional state detection

BIF maps the visitor's interaction pattern against its 7 emotional states. Pace, calm, fire, intuition — each state carries a different quality of intent.

03 / SCORE

Resonance score

A resonance score is generated. High resonance visitors are in a state aligned with your brand frequency. Low resonance signals mismatch — save your retargeting budget.

04 / META

Pixel intelligence

The resonance signal feeds your Meta pixel. Your lookalike audiences, retargeting, and conversion campaigns now target emotional matches — not just behavioral ones.

The seven signals

BIF reads your visitors
in seven frequencies.

Each emotional state carries a different signal about lead quality. A visitor in Intuition state — curious, open, sensing something without being able to name it — is worth ten visitors in Danger state, who are defensive, price-comparing, ready to leave.

Pace

Deep presence. Stillness.

Visitor is absorbing. Long dwell time, minimal scrolling. This is someone who reads everything.

4–6 BPM

Calm

Clarity. Open decision mode.

Visitor is evaluating with a clear head. High quality lead signal — they are in conditions to decide.

8–12 BPM

Night

Depth seeking. Searching for truth.

Visitor is in research mode, digging into your story. Early funnel — high potential if nurtured right.

4–6 BPM

Fire

Urgency. Action ready.

Visitor is hot — rapid navigation, direct intent. Ready to convert. Retarget immediately.

24–30 BPM

Intuition

Sensing something. Resonating.

Visitor can feel your brand frequency without naming it. The highest emotional alignment signal Honey detects.

16–20 BPM

Danger

Resistance. Defensive browsing.

Visitor is comparing, skeptical, ready to exit. Low resonance. Exclude from retargeting — don't waste budget.

40–60 BPM

Warmth

Warmth. Emotional connection forming.

Visitor is developing an emotional bond with the brand. Highest conversion potential over a 7-day window.

8–14 BPM
